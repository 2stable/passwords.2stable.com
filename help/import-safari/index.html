How to export your data from Safari and import it into Password Manager by 2Stable?

If you want to export your saved passwords or credit cards from Safari, the process depends on whether you’re using a Mac or an iPhone/iPad. Follow the steps for your device to create an export file that can be imported into the Password Manager by 2Stable.

Follow the device-specific export instructions

Once you’ve completed the export on your device, the generated file can be imported into the Password Manager by 2Stable to manage all your passwords and credit cards in one secure place.
